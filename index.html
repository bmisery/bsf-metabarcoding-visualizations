Distribution of Potentially Pathogenic Bacteria Across Insect Rearing and Processing Matrices

This dataset and its visualizations support the article:

B. Misery, L. Brulé, R. Djema, X. Yan, V. Le Cozic, G. Baudouin, M. Federighi, and G. Boué. Investigating the microbial dynamics of Hermetia illucens powder throughout rearing and processing: An integrated approach using cultural and metabarcoding methods. Foods, 2025, 14, x. [Link to journal]

Interactive sunburst plots were generated using Plotly to visualize the relative taxonomic composition (up to the genus level) of the microbial communities identified in different matrices.

  • Egg sample
  • Larvae D5 and D14 samples
  • Substrates samples
  • Frass samples
  • Processed larvae samples
  • Powder samples
